Vanilla Milkshake

Description

This an article showing the reader how to make a delicious recipe like the one shown above. We know that our famous recipe we will leave your taste buds more than satisfied!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Whole Milk
  • 2 Tablespoons ofIce Cream
  • 1/4 Cup of Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Cup of Cinnamon
  • Blender

Steps

  1. Pour milk and ice cream into the blender.
  2. Allow blender to mix the products for 30 seconds.
  3. Now add the vanilla extract and cinnamon.
  4. Softly blend the mix.
  5. Pour into cup and enjoy!
