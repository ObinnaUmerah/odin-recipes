Spaghetti

Description

This is the recipe for making the traditional spaghetti dish. It is very simple and can be made very fast and effortlessly.

Ingredients

  • A box of spaghetti
  • A jar of marinara sauce
  • 1-2 pounds of ground beef
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Fill up a large pot with water and a handful of salt. Begin to boil the water.
  2. Take the meat out of the container and set it in a large pot. Put the pan on the stove and set the heat to medium.
  3. Mix the marinara sauce with the cooked meat. Take the jar of marinara sauce and pour all of it on the cooked meat.
  4. Once the water has come to a boil, throw the spaghetti in. It should cook for 7-11 minutes.
  5. Drain the noodles and then place the noodles in the sauce. Stir together very well and enjoy!
